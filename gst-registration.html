Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GST registration in India.

Is GST registration mandatory? +

Yes, GST registration is mandatory if your annual turnover exceeds ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for special category states like J&K, Himachal Pradesh, etc.). It is also mandatory if you sell goods or services interstate or through ecommerce platforms like Amazon or Flipkart — regardless of turnover.

How long does GST registration take? +

GST registration typically takes 3–7 working days after complete document submission. In some cases where the officer raises queries, it may take slightly longer, but our team handles all queries on your behalf promptly.

What is the fee for GST registration? +

The government fee for GST registration is zero — it is completely free to register on the GST portal. Eleven IX charges a nominal professional fee for document preparation, application filing, and follow-up. Contact us at contactadmin11@gmail.com for exact pricing.

Can I register GST without a physical office? +

Yes, you can register for GST using your home address as the principal place of business. Alternatively, a virtual office with a valid address proof and NOC from the landlord is also accepted by the GST department.

What happens if I don’t register for GST? +

Failure to register for GST when required can result in heavy penalties (10% of tax due, minimum ₹10,000), interest charges on unpaid tax, and potential legal action. Additionally, unregistered businesses cannot claim Input Tax Credit (ITC), meaning they pay more tax on all business purchases.
