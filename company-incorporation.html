Our Incorporation Process

From name approval to certificate of incorporation — we handle everything end-to-end, 100% online.

1

Name Approval (MCA)

We search for name availability on the MCA portal and file for name approval through SPICe+ or RUN form, ensuring your proposed company name is unique and compliant.

2

DSC & DIN Generation

Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) and Director Identification Number (DIN) are generated for all proposed directors as required by MCA regulations.

3

MOA & AOA Drafting

Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA) are professionally drafted, defining your company’s objectives and governance structure.

4

Incorporation Filing

The complete incorporation application is filed on the MCA portal via the SPICe+ form along with all supporting documents for Registrar of Companies (RoC) approval.

5

PAN & TAN Allotment

Company PAN and Tax Deduction Account Number (TAN) are automatically allotted through the integrated SPICe+ process at no additional government cost.

6

Certificate of Incorporation

The RoC issues your digital Certificate of Incorporation along with your Company Identification Number (CIN). Your company is now legally incorporated!

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about company incorporation in India.

What is the minimum capital for a Private Limited Company? +

There is no minimum paid-up capital requirement for a Private Limited Company after the Companies Act 2013 amendment. You can technically start with just ₹1 as paid-up capital, making it accessible for all entrepreneurs.

How many directors are needed? +

A Private Limited Company requires a minimum of 2 directors (maximum 15). An OPC (One Person Company) requires just 1 director. An LLP requires a minimum of 2 designated partners. At least one director must be an Indian resident in all cases.

Can NRIs incorporate a company in India? +

Yes, NRIs and foreign nationals can be directors and shareholders of an Indian company with some additional documentation such as a notarized and apostilled passport copy. However, at least one director must be an Indian resident (someone who has stayed in India for at least 182 days in the previous calendar year).

What is DIN? +

Director Identification Number (DIN) is a unique 8-digit identification number issued by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) to every person intending to become a director of a company in India. It is mandatory for all directors before filing for incorporation.

How long does company incorporation take? +

Company incorporation typically takes 10–15 working days after the submission of all complete and correct documents. This includes time for name approval, DSC generation, form preparation, and RoC processing. Our team ensures smooth filing to avoid unnecessary delays.
