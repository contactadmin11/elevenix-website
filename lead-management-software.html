FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Eleven IX Lead Management Software. Click any question to expand the answer.

How is lead management software different from CRM? ▼
Lead management focuses on the top of the funnel — capturing and qualifying new leads. CRM covers the full customer lifecycle including post-sale relationship management, invoicing and retention. Together they form a complete sales system.
Can it integrate with Facebook and Google Ads? ▼
Yes, leads from Facebook Lead Ads and Google Ads can be automatically captured into the system in real time — no manual copy-pasting from ad manager.
Does it work for a team of salespeople? ▼
Yes, you can manage a full sales team, assign leads individually or by round-robin, track each person's performance, set monthly targets and generate team reports.
Can I track which source is giving the best leads? ▼
Yes, detailed analytics show lead quality, conversion rates and ROI by source — so you know whether your best leads come from Facebook, IndiaMart, Google or organic traffic.
Is WhatsApp integration available? ▼
Yes, WhatsApp Business API integration is available for automated follow-up messages, lead acknowledgements and appointment reminders — directly from the platform.
