FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Eleven IX GST Billing Software. Click any question to expand.

Is the software compliant with latest GST rules? ▼
Yes, our software is always updated to the latest GST council notifications and GSTN requirements. Your business stays compliant automatically.
Can I file GSTR returns directly from the software? ▼
Yes, the software generates GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B JSON files that can be directly uploaded to the GSTN portal. This eliminates manual data entry and reduces filing errors significantly.
Does it support multiple GSTIN? ▼
Yes, you can manage multiple businesses and GSTINs from a single account — ideal for business owners with more than one registered entity or branch.
What is E-Invoicing and do I need it? ▼
E-Invoicing is mandatory for businesses with turnover above ₹5 crores. Our software supports IRN generation directly through the Invoice Registration Portal (IRP), so your invoices are always valid and compliant.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes, all data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols and stored on secure servers with daily automated backups. Your financial data is always protected.
